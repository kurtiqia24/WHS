WHS STEM Academy

ABOUT US
The Walpole High School STEM Academy inspires young people to be future leaders by engaging them in real world, hands-on, mentor-based STEM activities and curriculum. We teach STEM as an integrated program and carefully connect the curriculum in each of the STEM fields throughout the duration of the Academy. Our programs build Science and Math skills inspire innovation and creative thinking, and foster well-rounded life capabilities including resiliency, teamwork, and communication skills essential in today's hi-tech society. The curriculum includes courses rich in the application of content, extensive problem-solving experiences, and integrated technologies. Advanced Placement and/or Honors courses in STEM are incorporated into the curriculum in order to challenge our students throughout their high school career and to prepare them for the rigors of STEM fields of study in college and beyond.
